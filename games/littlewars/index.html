LittleWars is a 2D strategy game, written by Miguel Gonzalez.

About

Hundreds of robots want to conquer your planet. Defend your nation with strong soldiers, build factories and explore big maps.

Inspiration

I love games like Command&Conquer, where you have all freedom to build a small base to have defense against other players. It's been a long time ago when I programmed this game. Therefore, many bugs (still) exist. The game has over 20.000 lines of code, excuse me for gameplay bugs!
